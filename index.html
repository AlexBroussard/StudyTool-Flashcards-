What is generative AI?
Generative AI are models that create new content—text, images, or audio—by learning patterns from large datasets. They use deep learning architectures like transformers and diffusion models.

What is a diffusion model?
Diffusion models generate images by starting from noise and iteratively denoising to produce a realistic image; they are widely used for high-quality image synthesis.

What is a transformer?
A transformer is a neural network architecture that uses self-attention to model relationships between tokens; it’s the backbone of many modern language models.
